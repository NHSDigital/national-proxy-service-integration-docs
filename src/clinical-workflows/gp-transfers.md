One of the key problems that the National Proxy Service seeks to address is continuity of care. Historically when a patient or proxy moves GP practice proxy access has been lost and must be requested and processed again. By introducing the national service, supplier systems can prompt GPs to review and - where appropriate - enable proxy access once a GP transfer has completed.

The national service supports this by enabling the new GP to view existing authorised proxy roles for the patient and subsequently assign appropriate permissions. This avoids the proxy or patient having to re-request access and provide information to support checks that have already been performed.
